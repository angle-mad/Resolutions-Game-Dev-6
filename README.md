# Resolution Game Dev Week 5 Project

## How to Play
Move water guy left with a, move right with d, jump up with a
move fire guy left with left arrow key, move right with right arrow key, jump up with up arrow key

## Description
One player controls water guy and the other controls fire guy with their respective controls. Your goal is to reach the flag at the end of the level with both characters. Lava will kill the water guy but the fire guy can walk straight through it. Water will kill the fire guy but the water guy can walk straight through it. Only the fire guy can move fire boxes.
